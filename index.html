Who We Are

A Trusted Partner for the Nonprofit Sector

Proth Consulting was founded on a simple belief: nonprofits deserve the same caliber of strategic and operational support as any high-performing organization. We bring decades of sector experience to every engagement — without the overhead of a large firm.

Whether you need a thought partner for long-range planning, an experienced fundraiser to build your development program, or a seasoned executive to lead your team through transition, we meet you where you are.

  • Deep nonprofit sector expertise across mission types and budget sizes
  • Practical, results-oriented engagements tailored to your organization
  • Flexible models — project, fractional, or interim — that fit your needs and budget
  • A commitment to your long-term capacity, not dependency on outside help
